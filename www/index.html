My App

I don't know how awesome this app is but I've spent quite a bit of time to get it to work in a style similar to the material design language Google introduced in Lollipop. I chose to go this route after researching the various UI guidelines for Barb's class last week.

The exiting page should fly-out to the right and the new page will fade in just as the exiting page reaches the edge of the screen.

Hopes & Difficulties

Clicking on each tab results in the bar below transitioning to the active tab and the opacity of the active tab changes as well. I spent an ungodly amount of time working on the timing of transitions and I'm still not sure they're working they way I want. It does work though.
